"Have no fear of perfection - you’ll never reach it." - Salvador Dali, Surrealist

Ivy D

Designer & Owner

For the past two years, I've been developing my graphic design skills under the alias, Kittanie. What started as a simple art request from a friend has developed into a steady stream of freelance work for clients ranging from online entertainers to businesses. I use the full Adobe CS, from After Effects to Dimension, to create high-quality branding packages, complete with animations. What you see here is my portfolio of finished products, created by my passion and self-discipline.

Anybody can make changes and persue their passions; it's never too late to grow.
